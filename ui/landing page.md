## Design System: "Zen Glass"

The aesthetic must evoke a deep sigh of relief. It should feel like stepping into a perfectly organized, quiet room. We are abandoning "hacker vibes" for a mature, sophisticated, editorial look.

**Color Palette:**

- **Background:** Soft, warm, off-white (`#fcfcfc` or `#faf9f8`). Avoid blinding, stark white.
- **Surfaces:** Floating elements use a very soft glassmorphism. Not heavy shadows, just delicate, frosted diffusion that blends gently into the background.
- **Accents:** Muted lavender (`#a78bfa` with low saturation) and sage green (`#86efac`). These colors don't shout; they gently signal status.
- **Typography:** Deep charcoal (`#2d3748`) instead of harsh black.
  - _Headings:_ Elegant, wide Geometric Sans (e.g., _Outfit_, _Inter Display_).
  - _Body:_ A highly readable, quiet serif or clean sans (e.g., _Lora_ or _Geist_).

**Motion & Animation:**

- **The Breathing Rhythm:** Elements don't snap or pop. They "breathe." A 4-second slow, easing cycle for the recording indicator.
- **Fluid Entrances:** As you scroll, text fades in softly, drifting upward by just a few pixels.
- **Cinematic Pacing:** The page shouldn't feel crowded. Generous whitespace (negative space) is our most important design element.

## The "Good Vibe" Development Checklist

To achieve this mature, calm feeling natively, ensure the following are maintained in the codebase:

1. **Easing Functions**: Never use default `ease`. Use custom spring or cubic-bezier functions `(0.25, 1, 0.5, 1)` for all transitions. Everything must float, never snap.
2. **Typography Weights**: Avoid pure black text on pure white. Use `text-slate-800` for headings and `text-slate-500` for body copy. Let the text breathe with a generous `line-height` (1.6 or higher).
3. **Paddings & Margins**: Double whatever space you think you need. A calm UI is an empty UI.
4. **Accessible Harmony**: Ensure all buttons and text have minimum contrast. A relaxing app doesn't make you squint. ARIA labels ensure the app is calm and cooperative for screen-reader users too.
5. **Silent Errors**: Never show a bright red error box. If a microphone disconnects, show a gentle, amber notification: _"We lost the microphone. Take your time to reconnect it."_
