Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Sovereignty

BlueArkive is built on the principle of absolute data sovereignty. Your notes, transcripts, and AI-processed content are stored locally on your device by default. We never access, read, or mine your content.

2. What We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and hashed password (if you create an account).
  • Payment data: Processed by Razorpay (INR) and Lemon Squeezy (USD). We do not store payment details.
  • Device metadata: Device ID and platform (for Cloud Sync features only).
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash telemetry via Sentry (can be disabled).

3. Cloud Sync

If you opt into Cloud Sync, your data is encrypted end-to-end before leaving your device. We cannot decrypt your synced content.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and account management.
  • PiyAPI: Cloud AI queries (Pro/Team tiers only, opt-in).
  • Razorpay / Lemon Squeezy: Payment processing.

5. Your Rights

You can export all your data, delete your account, and revoke Cloud Sync access at any time from within the application.

6. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@bluearkive.com
