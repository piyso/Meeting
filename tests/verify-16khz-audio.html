🎤 16kHz Audio Configuration Verification

Task 10.2: Implement 16kHz resampling

📋 Test Purpose

This test verifies that the audio capture system is correctly configured for 16kHz sample rate, which is required by Whisper for optimal transcription quality.

What this test validates:

  • ✅ AudioContext is created with 16kHz sample rate
  • ✅ AudioWorklet processor receives 16kHz audio
  • ✅ Audio chunks sent to main process have 16kHz sample rate
  • ✅ Whisper will receive properly formatted audio

⚠️ Important Notes

  • This test requires microphone permission
  • You will need to speak into your microphone during the test
  • The test may take up to 30 seconds to complete
  • Some browsers may resample audio automatically if 16kHz is not supported
