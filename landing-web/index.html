FEATURES

Everything happens
on your machine.

Real-time Transcription

Whisper Turbo runs locally on your GPU. System audio + microphone captured simultaneously. No internet needed.

AI-Powered Notes

Qwen 2.5 LLM expands your bullet points into full meeting notes. One shortcut: Ctrl+Enter. All on-device.

Semantic Search

Find any moment from any meeting. Search by meaning, not just keywords. "What did we decide about pricing?"

Zero Data Leaks

E2E encrypted. AES-256. Your recordings never leave your machine. Not even we can access them.

Smart Hardware Adapt

Auto-detects your Mac's RAM and selects the optimal AI model. 8GB to 64GB — it just works.

Auto-Updates

Seamless background updates. New AI models and features delivered automatically via GitHub Releases.

HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
Zero complexity.

01

Download & Install

275MB download. Open the DMG, drag to Applications. AI models download automatically on first launch.

02

Start Recording

Hit ⌘+Shift+Space or click Record. BlueArkive captures system audio and microphone simultaneously.

03

Search & Expand

Every word is searchable. Select a note, hit Ctrl+Enter — AI expands it into polished meeting notes.
