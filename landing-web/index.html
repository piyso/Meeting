Invisible Capture

BlueArkive captures macOS system audio and your microphone simultaneously from the background. No virtual cables, no clunky bots joining your Zoom calls, no "this meeting is being recorded" announcements from third parties.

  • ✓ Native macOS Audio Capture (CoreAudio)
  • ✓ 30-second chunking for real-time streaming
  • ✓ Hardware-accelerated Whisper Turbo

Semantic Search

Powered by local MiniLM embeddings. Search your entire meeting history by meaning, not just exact keywords.

AI Expansion

Type a rough note, press `Cmd+Enter`, and local Qwen 2.5 expands it using the exact surrounding transcript context.

Smart Hardware Scaling

BlueArkive automatically detects your system memory and deploys the appropriate AI models. On an M-Series Mac with 16GB RAM, expect sub-second transcription latency. On older machines, it gracefully scales to lighter models without crashing.
