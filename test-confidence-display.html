Usage Guidelines

For Users: Confidence scores help you identify which transcript segments may need review or editing. Focus on red badges (low confidence) first, then yellow badges (medium confidence) if time permits. Green badges (high confidence) are generally accurate and rarely need editing.

For Developers: The confidence value comes from the ASR engine (Whisper or Moonshine) and ranges from 0.0 to 1.0. The display component automatically converts this to a percentage and applies the appropriate color-coding based on the defined thresholds.
